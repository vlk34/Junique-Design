About us

What is Junique?

- 01

Junique is a car design studio that started its operations in 2010.

The words "Japanese" and "unique" are combined to form the name Junique. Which means "Unique Japanese".

Our Story

- 02

Erdem Kuskun's passion in Japanese culture and automobiles was the starting point for everything. Junique does more than only modify Japanese vehicles.

The team also makes Japanese-style modifications on Italian and German cars. Junique doesn't just modify Japanese automobiles. The team also gives German and Italian cars a Japanese makeover.
